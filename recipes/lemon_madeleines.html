Lemon Madeleines

This recipe will yield 6 savoury Lemon Madeleines

Ingredients

Lemon Madeleines:

  • Whole Eggs: 45g
  • Milk: 6g
  • Lemon Zest: 4g
  • Sugar: 42g
  • Honey: 6g
  • Cake Flour: 62g
  • Baking Powder: 2.9g
  • Pinch of Salt
  • Melted Butter: 56g

Lemon Icing:

  • Fresh Lemon Juice: 6g
  • Powdered Sugar: 30g~36g

Lemon Pipette:

  • Fresh Lemon Juice: 40g
  • Water: 10g
  • Sugar: 40g
