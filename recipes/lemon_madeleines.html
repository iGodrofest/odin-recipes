Lemon Madeleines

This recipe will yield 6 savoury Lemon Madeleines

Ingredients

Lemon Madeleines:

  • Whole Eggs: 45g
  • Milk: 6g
  • Lemon Zest: 4g
  • Sugar: 42g
  • Honey: 6g
  • Cake Flour: 62g
  • Baking Powder: 2.9g
  • Pinch of Salt
  • Melted Butter: 56g

Lemon Icing:

  • Fresh Lemon Juice: 6g
  • Powdered Sugar: 30g~36g

Instructions

Lemon Madeleines:

  1. Pre-heat your oven at 190ºC.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the whole eggs and the lemon zest until well combined.
  3. Add the milk for better results the eggs and the milk should be at room temperature.
  4. Add the sugar and honey. Whisk until the sugar has dissolved and the mixture is smooth.
  5. Mix until well dissolved whole eggs and milk should be at room temperature.
  6. Sift in the flour and baking powder.
  7. Gently fold until incorporated do not overmix.
  8. Add the melted butter make sure it's between 50~60ºC and fold until the batter is smooth and gloosy.
  9. Transfer the batter to a piping bag for easier filling.
  10. Pipe the batter into the prepared madeleine mold, fill each cavity about 3/4 full.
  11. Bake it for 9~10mins at 180ºC
  12. Remove from the oven and let the madeleines cool in the mold for a few minutes.

Lemon Icing:

  1. In a small bowl combine the powdered sugar and the lemon juice.
  2. Mix until smooth and pourable.
  3. Using a pastry brush, gently coat the cooled madeleines with the icing.
  4. Place the iced madeleines back in the oven at low heat (about 100ºC) for 2~3min to help set the glaze.
