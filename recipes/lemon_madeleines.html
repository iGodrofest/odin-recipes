Lemon Madeleines

This recipe will yield 6 savoury Lemon Madeleines

Ingredients

Lemon Madeleines:

  • Whole Eggs: 45g
  • Milk: 6g
  • Lemon Zest: 4g
  • Sugar: 42g
  • Honey: 6g
  • Cake Flour: 62g
  • Baking Powder: 2.9g
  • Pinch of Salt
  • Melted Butter: 56g

Lemon Icing:

  • Fresh Lemon Juice: 6g
  • Powdered Sugar: 30g~36g

Lemon Pipette:

  • Fresh Lemon Juice: 40g
  • Water: 10g
  • Sugar: 40g

Instructions

Lemon Madeleines:

  1. Pre-heat oven at 190ºC
  2. On a bowl add the eggs and the lemon zest
  3. Add to the bowl milk
  4. Then add sugar and honey
  5. Mix until well dissolved whole eggs and milk should be at room temperature
  6. Add sifted flour and baking powder
  7. Mix well
  8. Add butter for better results butter should be at around 50ºC~60ºC
  9. When everything looks well mixed put it onto a pastery bag
  10. Put it onto a madeleine mold
  11. Bake it for 9~10mins at 180ºC
  12. Let cool off

Lemon Icing:

  1. Put powdered sugar into a bowl
  2. Add the Fresh lemon Juice
  3. Mix well
  4. With a brush, gently coat the Madeleines
  5. Place it in the oven for a few minutes to solidify coating
